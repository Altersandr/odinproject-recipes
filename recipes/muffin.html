Chocolate chip muffin cake

Description

A muffin is an individually portioned baked product, however the term can refer to one of two distinct items:

a part-raised flatbread that is baked and then cooked on a griddle, or an quickbread that is chemically leavened and then baked in a mold.

Ingredients

  • 00 flour
  • Almond milk
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder for cakes
  • Dark chocolate chips
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanillin

Steps

  1. To prepare the almond milk and chocolate chip muffins, put the softened butter in a large bowl, add the sugar and mix with electric whips until the mixture is thick and creamy.

  2. Add one egg at a time and incorporate it into the dough with an electric whisk; wait until each egg is completely blended before adding another.

  3. Pour the sifted flour into the bowl, the yeast, vanillin and almond milk and mix everything with the electric whisk

  4. At this point, add almost all the chocolate chips and mix with a spatula to incorporate them evenly into the mixture; the rest of the chocolate chips will be used to garnish the muffins. Transfer the mixture obtained into a sac-à-poche without a nozzle.

  5. Line the muffin mold with the paper cups and fill them with the mixture for about three quarters. Decorate the surface with the chocolate chips you have kept aside and bake in a preheated static oven at 180 ° for about 20 minutes. After the cooking time, do the toothpick test and bake your delicious muffins with almond milk and chocolate chips!
